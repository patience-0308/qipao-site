Brand Introduction
Shanghai Huamei Hantang Qipao Studio

Rooted in tradition and craftsmanship, Shanghai Huamei Hantang Qipao Studio has been a beloved name in custom qipao tailoring for over 30 years. With the guiding vision of "bringing traditional Chinese elegance to life" and "helping every woman feel confident and beautiful," the studio is dedicated to creating exquisite, made-to-measure qipaos that blend timeless style with personal charm.

At the heart of the studio is a master tailor with over 60 years of experience — a true national treasure in the world of hand-sewn garments. Every qipao is meticulously crafted by hand, beginning with expert pattern-making and cutting that enhances the wearer’s natural shape, offering both comfort and elegance.

Signature elements such as the traditional straight toggle buttons are all hand-stitched, preserving authentic techniques. Customers can also choose from a collection of intricately handmade flower buttons, designed to complement the fabric and add a striking finishing touch.

Praised by clients around the world, Shanghai Huamei Hantang Qipao Studio has become the premier destination for custom wedding qipaos and a symbol of refined Chinese artistry.
